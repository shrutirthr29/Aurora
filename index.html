Who can participate in the Hackathon?

Aurora is open to all undergraduates around the region. There are no prerequisites for the participants. Anyone with an inquisitive mind can participate.

What is the registration Procedure?

Registrations will take place at Devfolio. One has to fill out a form to register for the event.

Is there any Registration fee ?

It will cost nothing but your time and efforts.

By whom and when will we be given the problem statements?

The problem statements will be provided during the Orientation Session by the speakers of the event.

What is the team size for the hackathon?

Teams need to have 3 to 5 members when submitting a project.

Do I will have rights of my own code?

Definitely! You do not transfer any of your ownership rights when you submit the code. It is just used for evaluation and award purposes.

Will there be certificates/goodies provided to the participants?

Yes, participation certificates and goodies will be rewarded to all the participants.

How can we get updates?

For updates, you can visit our social media platforms. Links to them are provided here itself.

Will there be food arrangements?

Yes, there will be proper meals provided.
